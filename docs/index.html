What is SparrOS?

SparrOS is an operating system, based on Runtu that is designed to bring out the best of computer users and offer an operating system preinstalled with what everybody wants. It includes many programs such as multiple web browsers for the family, games for him, social media for her, emulation for us, office for the business man, development platforms for the coders, and much more. German, English, Russian, French, Japanese, and Italian is supported.


Raspberry Pi Compatible!

We now have a Raspberry Pi flavor of SparrOS under construction. To download the image, visit this page. Supported Raspberry Pi models: RPi 1, RPi 2, RPi Zero. BananaPi and OrangePi not yet supported.
